# E-commerce Sales Performance Analysis (2024)

## 📌 Overview
This project analyzes the sales performance of an e-commerce platform across multiple regions, product categories, and sales channels in 2024. The goal is to uncover key revenue drivers, customer behavior patterns, and potential risks to support strategic decision-making.

## 🎯 Business Objective
To evaluate platform performance, identify top revenue drivers, and assess risks in order to guide decisions around product strategy, market focus, and revenue diversification. To identify key revenue drivers, customer behavior patterns, and operational risks affecting overall business performance.

## 📊 Dataset Overview
The dataset contains 10,000 transactions recorded in 2024, covering multiple platforms, product categories, and geographic regions.

**Key variables include:**
- Revenue  
- Number of items per order  
- Product category and brand  
- Sales platform (Jumia, Amazon, Souq)  
- Customer ratings  
- Purchase date (monthly, quarterly, weekly trends)  
- Geographic location  

## 📈 Overall Sales Performance
- Total Revenue: $302 million  
- Total Transactions: 10,000  
- Average Items per Order: 3  
- Average Item Price: $10,000  

The platform demonstrated stable transaction volume and consistent purchasing patterns throughout the year.

## 🛒 Platform Performance
Among all sales channels:
- **Souq** generated the highest revenue  

This suggests stronger customer engagement and market penetration on Souq, making it the most critical platform for future growth and investment.

## 📦 Product & Brand Performance
- **Top Brand:** Xiaomi  
- **Top Product:** Redmi Note 12  
- Revenue Contribution: $34 million  

This indicates strong customer preference for Xiaomi products, likely driven by affordability and perceived value.

## 🧩 Category Analysis
- **Top Category:** Electronics  

Electronics significantly outperformed Fashion and other categories, indicating that demand is heavily skewed toward functional and technology-driven products.

## 🌍 Geographic Insights
- **Top Location:** Cairo  

Cairo recorded the highest sales volume, making it the primary demand hub. This may be driven by higher population density, purchasing power, and stronger logistics infrastructure.

## 📅 Time-Based Trends
- **Peak Quarter:** Q3  
- **Top Month:** July  
- Q3 Revenue: $78 million  

Sales peaked despite price increases, suggesting relatively **price-inelastic demand** for high-performing products.

## 📆 Weekly Trends
- **Top Sales Day:** Wednesday  

Mid-week sales peaks suggest an opportunity to strategically time promotions and campaigns for maximum impact.

## ⭐ Customer Satisfaction
- Average Rating: 3.0  

Despite strong revenue performance, customer satisfaction is moderate, indicating potential issues with product quality, delivery, or after-sales service.

## ⚠️ Risk Analysis
Revenue is highly concentrated in:
- One brand (Xiaomi)  
- One product (Redmi Note 12)  
- One platform (Souq)  

This creates **dependency risk**, making the business vulnerable to disruptions affecting these key drivers.

## ✅ Recommendations
- Diversify product offerings beyond top-performing brands  
- Expand and optimize underperforming sales channels (Jumia, Amazon)  
- Improve customer experience to increase satisfaction and retention  
- Leverage high-performing periods (Q3, Wednesdays) for targeted campaigns  
- Strengthen presence in high-demand regions like Cairo
